# Data Dictionary

## 01_fund_master
Contains master information about mutual funds.

## 02_nav_history
Stores historical Net Asset Value (NAV) data.

## 03_aum_by_fund_house
Stores Assets Under Management (AUM) by fund house.

## 04_monthly_sip_inflows
Stores monthly SIP inflow information.

## 05_category_inflows
Stores category-wise mutual fund inflows.

## 06_industry_folio_count
Stores folio counts across the industry.

## 07_scheme_performance
Stores performance metrics and returns of schemes.

## 08_investor_transactions
Stores investor purchase and redemption transactions.

## 09_portfolio_holdings
Stores portfolio holdings of mutual fund schemes.

## 10_benchmark_indices
Stores benchmark index information.
